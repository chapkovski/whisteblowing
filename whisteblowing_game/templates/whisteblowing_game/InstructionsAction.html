Each of the three players not selected as Taker is a potential Observer. Each potential observer decides between three actions:

  • Abstain: The Observer does not intervene. The Taker keeps {{ earnings_thief }} points. The Observer and the two Bystanders receive {{ Constants.personal_endowment }} points.
  • Report: The Observer reveals the Taker to the rest of the group. Thereafter, the Observer and the two Bystanders can individually decide to Sanction the Taker (see Stage 3).
  • Sanction: The Observer reduces the payoff of the Taker by 24 points (from 26 points to 2 points). The Observer pays a cost of 6 points. The Observer's final payoff is 2 points. The payoffs of the two Bystanders are unaffected.

After each of the three players has decided, one of them is randomly selected as actual Observer. The other two players are Bystanders. Only the decision of the actual Observer is implemented. The decisions of two Bystanders remain inconsequential.
