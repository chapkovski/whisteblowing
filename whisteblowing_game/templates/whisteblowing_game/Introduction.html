Instructions (Page 1 of 3)

In this study, your income depends on your own decision, as well as the decisions of your group members. Throughout the study you will earn points. These points are converted into Dollars at the end of the study at a rate of 1 point = x Dollars.
In this study, you play in group of 4 players for 15 rounds. The group composition is constant for the entire study. In each round, each player starts with 8 points as personal endowment. In addition, there is a common pool of 36 points.
