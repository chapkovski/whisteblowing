After the decisions of the Taker and the Observer are revealed, the two players being neither Taker nor Observer can send up to two Deduction or Addition Points to the Observer. Sending one Deduction Point costs 1 point and reduces the payoff of the Observer by 3 points. Sending one Addition Point costs 1 point and increases the payoff of the Observer by 3 points.
