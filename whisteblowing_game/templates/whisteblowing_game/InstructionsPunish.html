Instructions

The Taker's payoff is reduced by 24 MUs if at least one of the three players selects Sanction. However, the Taker can only be sanctioned at a maximum of 24 MUs. If more than one player sanction, the Taker's payoff is still reduced only by 24 MUs. Each player has to pay a cost of 6 MU for selecting Sanction, irrespective of how many other players sanction. If none of the three players selects Sanction, the payoff of the Taker remains unaffected.
