If the Taker chose to Take and the Observer reported, each of the three players not selected as Taker decides again between Sanction and Abstain. In this case, the Taker's payoff is reduced by 24 points if at least one of the three players selects Sanction. Each player selecting Sanction has to pay a cost of 6 points, irrespective of how many other players sanction. However, the Taker can only be sanctioned once. If more than one player sanction, the Taker's payoff is still reduced only by 24 points. If none of the three players selects Sanction, the payoff of the Taker is unaffected.
