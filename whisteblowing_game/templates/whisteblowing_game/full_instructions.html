Stage 1: Take or Leave decision

At the beginning of each round, one randomly chosen group member (Taker) decides between two actions:

  • Take: Half of the common pool (18 points) is added to your personal income. The preliminary payoff of the Taker is 8 + 36/2 = 26 points. The other half of the common pool is destroyed. The payoff of the other players is 8 points.
  • Leave: The common pool is divided equally between the four players. Each player's payoff from this round is 8 + 36/4 = 17 points.
